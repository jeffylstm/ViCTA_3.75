The angle adjuster holds the phone clamp. The angle adjuster should be fixed through the cone boards using a 6mm bolt (approximately 3-4cm) and a wing nut to secure it.


Alternatives may be used if these are not available but the phone must remain still during recording. The use of flexible phone holders is definitely not recommended.
